About SoilSense

SoilSense is an AI-powered platform designed for farmers, agronomists, gardeners, and researchers who need fast, reliable soil moisture insights without expensive IoT sensors. Built using a Kaggle-sourced dataset of soil images across multiple moisture conditions, our model is trained to classify soil into Dry, Moderate, and Wet categories — helping you make smarter irrigation decisions that conserve water and improve crop yield.


Dataset: Soil Moisture Classification Dataset — Kaggle  |  🔗 kaggle.com/datasets/zubairhossain/soil-moisture-prediction-dataset
